Statements From The Green Planet

Statements From The Green Planet is a Sublime Records sampler released in 1997 that features 2 exclusive Silage tracks as well as My Car Makes Me Sin off Watusi. Statements was also an enhanced CD that included a Quicktime video for Watusi and other Sublime band videos and music.

Track List:
  1. Silage - Ragin' Chicken
  2. Silage - Ragin' Chicken (Trip Hop Mix)
  3. Silage - My Car Makes Me Sin
  4. Ruby Joe - Tounge is Numb
  5. Ruby Joe - Studio #5
  6. Quayle - The Pretender
  7. Quayle - True Blue
  8. Honey - Worn (Rome Mix)
  9. Honey - Evergreen
  10. Kosmos Express - Little Tree
  11. Kosmos Express - I Don't Have a Gun
  12. Fold Zandura - Return
